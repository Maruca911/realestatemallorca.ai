## About
Real Estate Mallorca is a free property matching service for the island of Mallorca, Spain. We connect international buyers, renters, and investors with vetted properties and agents. Users submit their preferences and receive 3 curated property options within 24 hours via email or WhatsApp.

## Primary Services
- Short-term vacation rentals (apartments, villas, holiday homes)
- Long-term residential rentals (monthly and annual leases)
- Villas for sale (luxury homes, modern properties, sea-view estates)
- Fincas for sale (traditional country estates, rural properties)
- Apartments for sale (modern flats, penthouses, beachfront units)
- Beachfront property (first-line sea-view homes across all types)
- Investment properties (buy-to-let, capital growth, Golden Visa eligible)
- Real estate agent matching (vetted, multilingual professionals)

## Market Data
- Average property price: 4,600 - 5,200 per sqm
- Apartment prices: 2,200 - 8,000 per sqm depending on location
- Short-term rental rates: 80 - 500 per night depending on location
- Long-term rental rates: 750 - 3,500 per month
- Rental yields: 4-8% for licensed tourist properties
- Annual appreciation: 3-8% depending on segment
- Purchase costs: 10-13% on top of purchase price
- Golden Visa threshold: 500,000 EUR minimum investment
- Mortgage rates: 2.5-3.5% fixed, Euribor +1.0-1.5% variable
- Non-resident LTV: 60-70%
